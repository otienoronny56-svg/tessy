Admin Operations Guide

Welcome to the NIC Command Center. This portal allows you to manage the Academy's digital presence in real-time. Follow the guidelines below to ensure a premium experience for our students.

1. Managing Courses

1

When to use:

Use the "Manage Courses" tab whenever you launch a new training program or need to update pricing/curriculum for existing ones.

2

The Data Package:

Our system packs Duration, Level, Price, and Description into a single entry. Use the "Icon representation" dropdown to match the course's focus (e.g., use the Palette icon for Latte Art).

2. Publishing to the Journal

1

Content Quality:

The Blog section (Journal) is for SEO and thought leadership. Aim for at least 300 words per post to improve Google rankings.

2

Images:

Always upload a high-resolution cover photo. Landscape orientation works best for the website's grid layout.

3. Faculty Updates

1

Maintaining Authority:

When adding faculty, ensure their Role (e.g., "Lead Barista Instructor") is clearly defined. This builds trust with potential students visiting the 'About' page.

Pro-Tips for Stability

  • Check the public site after every "Publish" to ensure layout looks correct.

  • Keep your headline titles under 50 characters to prevent text wrapping issues.

  • If an image fails to load, verify the file size is under 2MB.
